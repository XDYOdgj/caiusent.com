Premium Soy Wax Beads

Crafting with nature in mind? These premium soy wax beads are the perfect choice for any candle maker who values sustainability, safety, and quality. Made without paraffin or beeswax, they provide a cleaner burn and a long-lasting, fresh aroma for your home.

Thanks to their outstanding natural color retention, these beads help maintain vivid hues in your candles and resist frosting, giving you better control over the final look. They are ideal for a wide range of candle-making projects, including container candles, tealights, and wax melts, making them a versatile and reliable option for all your creations.

The smooth and creamy texture of these beads ensures effortless pouring and shaping, whether you're a beginner or an experienced crafter. Their microwavable and highly adaptable nature allows them to blend well with fragrances, wicks, and dyes, making your candles more appealing and satisfying to create.

Produced ethically from soybeans in the American Midwest, these wax beads support a greener and more responsible approach to crafting. Choose them for a better candle-making experience with a safer, healthier environment for you and your family.
